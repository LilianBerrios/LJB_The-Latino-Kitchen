Cuba Libre Recipe!

This is an original fmaily recipe that is very easy to make! This is one of my dad's regular favorites after a long hot day!

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 a can of Coca Cola (whichever version you prefer)
  • 2 ounces of white rum
  • Lime wedges
  • Ice

Steps:

  1. Fill half of your cup with ice.
  2. Squeeze in 3 lime wedges (or as many as needed) and drop in cup.
  3. Pour in rum.
  4. Top off the rest of your cup with coca cola or ~1/2 of the can.
  5. Stir well and taste. Add lime wedges to the brim if desired.

¡Buen provecho!
