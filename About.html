About Me

Who Am I?

Lilian Berrios was born in Houston, Texas and raised in Seattle, Washington. She attends Inglemoor High School as an IB students. Lilian comes from a mixed-latin background. Her mom is Argentinian and her dad is Puerto Rican. Ever since she was little, Lilian has loved to eat and try new foods. Especially foods from her cultural background. Lilian's closest friends are also Latino. Their families will trade recipes and invite each other over for big cookouts or "fiestas". Many of the recipes you'll find in this website are original family recipes, while others are recipes that her family uses frequently. Lilian plans to come back to The Latino Kitchen cooking website frequently over the next few years to add more delicious recipes.
