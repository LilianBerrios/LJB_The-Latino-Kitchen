Rum Punch Recipe!

Although this is not a family recipe, this drink can commonly be seen served at parties or for adults who need a second away from the sun! It's always very delicious and a personal favorite of my sister.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of orange juice
  • 1 cup of pineapple juice
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 1/4 cup rum
  • 1/4 cup dark rum
  • 1/2 cup of grenadine
  • Orange slices (optional)
  • Maraschino cherries (optional)

Steps:

  1. In a pitcher, combine the juices, the rum, and the grenadine.
  2. Stir and taste. Depeding on preferences, more lime juice may be necessary.
  3. Pour into ice-filled glasses. Serve with an orange slice or maraschino cherries, if desireds.

¡Buen provecho!

(Recipe is originally from Lemons For Lulu by Tanya Schroeder)
