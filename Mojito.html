Mojito Recipe!

Although this is not an originga family recipe, the adults in my family will use this recipe as a refrence each time they want to make a mojito. Except, in my family, we use many more limes!

Ingredients:

  • 10 fresh mint leaves
  • Lime wedges
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar (optional)
  • Ice
  • 1 1/2 ounces of white rum
  • 1/2 cup of club soda

Steps:

  1. Place mint leaves and 1 lime wedge into a sturdy glass. Use a muddler and crush to release mint oils and lime juice.
  2. Add 2 more lime wedges (or as needed) and 2 tablespoons sugar (optional), and muddle again to release the lime juice. Do not strain the mixture.
  3. Fill the glass either almost to the top with ice or simply add a hanful of ice to your glass (depending on the size of your glass).
  4. Pour in rum and fill the glass with club soda.
  5. Stir, taste, and add more sugar/limes/rum if desired.

¡Buen provecho!
