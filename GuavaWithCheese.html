Guava with Cheese Recipe!

The recipe is an original family recipe. Because this recipe isn't very hard to make, this is my abuelo's all-time favorite dessert and eats it almost every time as his dessert of the day.

Ingredients:

  • Guava paste
  • White cheese (hard)
  • Chocolate cream (syrup)

Steps:

  1. Cut the guava paste in your form of preference (circular, rectangular, etc.)
  2. Cut the white cheese in the same form and put the guava and cheese on top of each other or keep together with a toothpick.
  3. Lightly drizzle chocolate cream (syrup) over the cheese and guava, optional.

¡Buen provecho!
