Cheese Balls Recipe!

This is an original family recipe that is a very traditional appetizer in Puerto Rico. We usually dip the cheese balls in mayo-ketchup. It always ends up delicious!

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 pound of cheese, shredded (Gouda, Cheddar, or Swiss)
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • Bread or cracker crumbs
  • Vegetable oil

Steps:

  1. In the small bowl of an electric mixture, blend the cheese, egg, and cornstarch at a high speed.
  2. Take mixture by teaspoons, turn into balls, coat with crumbs and deep-fry in vegetable oil until golden brown.
  3. Remove and drain on absorbent paper. Serve immediately.
