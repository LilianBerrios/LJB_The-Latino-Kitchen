Culebrita Recipe!

This is an original family recipe my dad likes to drink whenever we are at the pool or beach in Puerto Rico. It's a refreshing drink that really shows the tropical side of drinks in Puerto Rico.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ounce of light rum
  • 1 ounce of dark rum
  • 1 ounce of peach snaps
  • 1 ounce of triple sec
  • 1 ounce of grand marnier
  • 4 ounces of orange juice
  • 2 ounces of pineapple juice
  • 0.5 ounsces of grenadine
  • Pineapple slices (optional)
  • Orange slices (optional)

Steps:

  1. Pour the juices and alcohol togther in a shaker. Shake well for 10-15 seconds.
  2. Pour mixture in a cup already filled with ice.
  3. Top glass with pineapple and orange slices, if desired.

¡Buen provecho!
